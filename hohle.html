HOHLE

Hohle is a roguelike shoot 'em up game there I developed on my own as a school project. As many others of its genre, the opbjective of the game s to survive a long as possible while hordes of enemies start spawning to try to kill the player. You can collect XP droped by defeated enemies to level up and unlock new abilities or improve the ones you already have at your disposal.

Key Features

You can play test this game Clicking Here

- Infinite enemy wave system that scales up in difficuty the further the player progresses on the level.
- 3 Diferent types of regular enemies with their own unique stats, and appearance.
- 1 Boss type enemy with their own health bar displayed on the players UI that appears after certain time has past on the game
- 2 Playable characters with different basic abilities but sharing base stats(HP and speed)
- 6 Types of weapons (3 per character) with their own unique characteristics and applications, including:
+ Katana.
+ Energy Ball.
+ Throwing Scythe.
+ Soul Blast.
+ Spining Axe.
+ Arcane Explosion.
> - Music and sound effect volume menu.
- Store menu that allows for the purchase of special items with In-game currency.
