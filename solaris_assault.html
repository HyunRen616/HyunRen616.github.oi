SOLARIS ASSAULT

Solaris Assault is a bullet hell game that i developed with a team of two other people. The game centers around trying to achieve the highest score possible surviving multiple waves of enemies with the help of an arsenal of special weapons that you wil have to manage carefully to maximise its efectivenes.

Key Features

You can play test this game Clicking Here

- Infinite enemy wave system that scales up in difficuty the further the player progresses on the level.
- 5 Diferent types of regular enemies with their own unique behaviour, stats, and appearance.
- 2 Boss type enemies with their own health bar displayed on the players UI.
- 7 Types of weapons with their own unique characteristics and applications, including:
+ Regular Canons.
+ Barage Misiles.
+ Guided Misiles.
+ Energy Shield.
+ EMP Pulse Attack.
+ Black Hole Projectie.
+ Dual Automatic Cannons.
- Music and sound effect volume menu.
- Tutorial page explayning the HUD and the controlers.
